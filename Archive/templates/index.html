Visualization for 2013 American Community Survey

This page is based on the data from 2013 American Community Survey which canbe download from here. This is an ongoing survey from the US Census Bureau and approximately 3.5 million households per year are asked detailed questions about who they are and how they live. Many topics are covered, including ancestry, education, work, transportation, internet use, and residency.
